About us

MOLK is a technology and information company focused on social inclusion, and in the SEDDA (Education System for the Hearing Impaired) project, turns its attention especially on the deaf at the school and academic environment. Founded at the Federal Institute of São Paulo (IFSP) in Caraguatatuba, in this project the company targets informing people about the history, the causes of deafness and the struggles of the hearing-impaired to then make our society more inclusive and aware about the subject.
